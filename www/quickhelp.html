General

Switching between the Playback panel and the Library is accomplished by tapping on the album cover or on any open area of the Playbar.

Library

The library provides Radio, Folder, Tag and Album views for browsing and selecting content and a Playbar for displaying song information and controlling playback.

Options for configuring various aspects of the Library including sort order, recently added lookback and more are located in the Appearance dialog which is accessible via the Main Menu.

A special search feature is available in Tag and Album views that accepts a year or year range as the search criteria for example 1995 or 1995-1998. It's activated by pressing the enter key or if on a touch device keying in !r.

Configuration

The Configuration screens are accessed via the Main Menu.

Special Buttons

Most buttons are self explanatory but several perform unique functions.

  • CoverView

    Display full screen CoverView screen saver. Tapping anywhere off of the Playbar dismisses CoverView.

  • Random

    Play random tracks from the Playlist (default) or the entire Music Library if Auto-shuffle is ON (see Audio Config).

  • Consume

    Remove the track from the Playlist after it finishes playing. Consume mode is automatically enabled when Auto-shuffle is ON.

  • Repeat

    Repeat the Playlist or if Single is enabled then repeat the track.

  • Single

    Used in conjunction with Repeat to repeat a single track.

  • Last track

    Toggle between the currently playing item and the previously played item.

  • Select random album

    Select a random Album for review when in Tag or Album views.

  • Play random album

    Instantly play a randomly selected album. This option performs Clear/Play or Add/Play depending on the Instant play action setting in Library options. If Instant play action is set to No action then this option defaults to Add/Play.

  • Favorite

    Add the currently selected track in the Playlist to the Favorites playlist. Playlists are listed in Library Folder view.

Active Elements

There are several non-button elements in the UI that perform an action when tapped.

  • Instant Play

    Tapping on the cover in Radio or Album views instantly plays the item by performing either a Clear/Play or Add/Play depending on the Instant play action setting in Library options. If the setting is Add/Play and the item is currently in the Playlist then it is played directly rather than adding a new duplicate item. There is also an option for No action.

  • Renderer Active screens

    Tapping on the title in the center of Bluetooth, Analog Input or S/PDIF Input Active will launch their respective Config screens which allow the input/output settings to be changed.

  • Time knob

    Tapping on the time while a song file is playing will toggle between counting the time up or down. Tapping or dragging the time ring moves forward or backward in the song.

  • Volume knob

    Tapping on the numeric volume level toggles the mute state. Tapping ahead on the volume ring advances the volume by a maximum number of steps called the Volume step limit. This limit can be set via Audio Config. Tapping the plus/minus buttons adjusts volume by 1 step.

  • Song title

    Tapping the song title underneath the cover art in Playback launches a Google search in a separate Browser tab. On Smartphones it scrolls the Playlist to that item.

  • Radio station or Artist - Album

    Tapping the Radio station name or the Artist - Album title underneath the cover art in Playback switches to Playbar in Radio or Tag view respectively. In tag view the artist filter is applied. In radio view the list is scrolled to the station.

  • Swipe gestures

    On Smartphones swiping up scrolls the Playlist into view and tapping on the Playbar cover returns to Playback. In the Library tag view swiping sideways shows/hides the Genres column.
